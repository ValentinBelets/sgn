Traffic Signs

Using this service

To search for a particular sign, click in the search box and enter a search term, then click the Search button.

You can search by sign name (e.g. stop), number (e.g. R1-1), or type (e.g. regulatory).

About this service

This sign register covers the standard signs used in NSW for regulating, warning, informing, and guiding road users. It specifies the sign classification, numbering, shape, and colour of each sign. Detailed design plans are also included for most signs. Some signs, such as the G Series, do not provide detailed designs as they are site specific. Refer to Australian Standard 1743, 1744 and 1742.2, and 1742.15 for further information.

More information

For matters relating to signage design or standards, please contact standards@transport.nsw.gov.au.

Transport's authorisation to councils for the use of prescribed traffic control devices can be found here: Transport's Authorisation and Delegation to Councils. Requests relating to use of restricted signs should be directed to the Transport for NSW representative on council's Local Transport Forum.

Enquiry forms and additional Transport for NSW contacts are available here.
